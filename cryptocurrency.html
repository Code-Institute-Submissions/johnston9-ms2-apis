CRYPTOCURRENCY

Cryptocurrencies are digital money, digital tools of exchange that use cryptography and the blockchain technology to facilitate secure and anonymous transactions. There had been several iterations of cryptocurrency over the years, but Bitcoin truly thrust cryptocurrencies forward in the late 2000s. There are thousands of cryptocurrencies floating out on the market now.

DATA

Like all trading and investing reading and understanding the data from trading site graphs and numbers is the cornerstone to all activity. In the world of cryptocurrency rise and fall more rapidly than any other commodity and trading crypto generally revolves around price speculation.

TRADING

Cryptocurrency is a highly volatile market so day trading is a high risk business. The price of a coin can rise or fall by more than 10%-50% in a single day. Fortunes can be lost or won. Knowing which coins to invest in for a long term investment plan is equally as risky or lucrative.

EXCHANGES

Most of the trading is done through exchanges where the price of a coin varies from exchange to exchange. When choosing an exchange there are other factors, apart from the price, like their security measures, prior security breach history and fees to take into consideration.
